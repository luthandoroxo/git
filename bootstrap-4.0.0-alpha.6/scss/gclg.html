WELCOME

Our vision is a safe world where all children and young people grow up with confidence and the opportunity to influence their own lives. Only when one cares about his own life and see a bright future can break negative patterns and exclusion .

So we work on an individual level to inspire, motivate and engage children and young people to take advantage of their inner power and potential through organized activity, sports and social activities. Learn more about Project Playground doing this .
